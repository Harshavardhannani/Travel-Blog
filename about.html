How It Began

A Journey Born from Passion

TravelBlog was founded in 1996 by two passionate explorers who fell in love with India's incredible diversity. What started as a personal travel journal quickly grew into India's most trusted travel platform.

We believe travel is more than visiting places — it's about connecting with cultures, creating bonds with locals, and returning home forever changed. Every tour we design carries this philosophy at its heart.

Today, our network of 8,000+ local guides and travel experts ensures every journey is authentic, safe, and unforgettable. From the snow-covered Himalayas to the sun-drenched beaches of Goa — we take you everywhere India's heart beats loudest.
